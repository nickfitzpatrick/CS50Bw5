Panoramic Highway to the top of Mount Tamlapais

This road involves windy roads through redwoods as you work your way up to one of the most breathtaking views in the Bay Area. Perfect when you want a cool coastal morning, or a spectacular sunset.

Best type of bike to bring:

Any

Best time of day to go:

Evening - Amazing sunsets over the ocean (or a white sea of low clouds) and avoids the morning fog and slippery roads

Type of ride:

Scenic, sporty
